Got questions?

What is car tuning?

Car tuning refers to the process of modifying a vehicle to improve its performance, handling, and aesthetics. This can involve adjustments to the engine, suspension, exhaust system, and more, to enhance the car's capabilities and driving experience.

How do you know which mods to use?

We start by discussing your goals and preferences, such as whether you‚Äôre looking for performance enhancements, improved handling, or cosmetic upgrades. After that, we evaluate your car‚Äôs current setup and provide recommendations based on our expertise, ensuring compatibility and optimal results.

Will tuning my car void the warranty?

It depends on the manufacturer and the specific modifications made. Some modifications can potentially void certain aspects of your warranty, especially if they affect the drivetrain or emissions systems. We recommend checking your warranty policy and discussing potential impacts with us before proceeding.

What performance upgrades do you offer?

We offer a wide range of upgrades, including ECU tuning, cold air intakes, exhaust system modifications, suspension upgrades, and aftermarket turbochargers or superchargers. We can also help with brake upgrades and wheel/tire installations for improved handling and aesthetics.

How much should I expect to spend on tuning my car?

The cost can vary significantly depending on the type and extent of modifications you choose. Basic ECU tuning can start at a few hundred dollars, while comprehensive performance upgrades can range from a couple thousand to several thousand dollars. We can provide a detailed estimate after assessing your specific needs

Will tuning my car affect fuel efficiency?

Tuning can have varying impacts on fuel efficiency. Some performance tunes can optimize fuel consumption, while others focused on power might lead to increased fuel usage. Our team can tailor tuning options to balance performance and efficiency based on your driving habits.
